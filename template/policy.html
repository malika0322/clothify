Return & Exchange Policy

We are happy to help with returns or exchanges Please read our policy below for more information

Eligibility for Return

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase, provided they are in unused, unwashed condition, with all original tags still attached If an item is faulty or damaged, we will accept a return or exchange

How to Return

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at budhathokimalika@gmail.com with your order details We will provide you with a return label and instructions

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 7-10 business days after the returned item is received and inspected

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, we will process your new item as soon as we receive the original product in return condition

Non-Returnable Items

Gift cards, sale items, and items marked as final sale are not eligible for return or exchange
